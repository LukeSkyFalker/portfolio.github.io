ABOUT ME

I am Luca Smaldone, also known as LukeSkyFalker, a young Italian who attended the I.P.S.E.O.A. "M. Pittoni" school in Pagani and graduated from high school. I have developed skills in the field of catering and hospitality, characterizing myself as a friendly and sociable person, able to build positive relationships with others. In my spare time, I cultivate a passion for advertising graphics, learning skills independently. My training path is constantly evolving, acquiring self-taught knowledge and information. I am open to new work experiences and I believe that my ability to adapt to new developments will positively contribute to any environment. I look for a rewarding future in which to put my skills into practice, in the knowledge that continuous learning is the basis of success.
